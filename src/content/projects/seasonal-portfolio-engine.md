This starter includes the stable theme contract, preference controls, and low-cost backdrop layer. Future foliage, petals, leaves, and ice effects should live in isolated experiment modules and communicate through the same season preference.

## Design goal

Keep the site readable and fast even when a visual system is disabled, unsupported, or intentionally reduced.
